New Food Allergy Program a Beacon of Hope

Parents of children with food allergies never get a day off. A cheese stick, a pretzel, a piece of candy could trigger a life-threatening reaction in an otherwise healthy child within a matter of seconds. Through our new Food Allergy Program, Children’s is committed to lessening that burden.

One in 59 children in the United States has Autism Spectrum Disorder, making autism more common than all childhood cancers, congenital heart defects, cystic fibrosis, juvenile diabetes and muscular dystrophy combined. This April join us in support of our community’s children and families affected by autism by celebrating National Autism Awareness Month.

As one of the largest providers of autism care in the country and a global leader in research and science, Marcus Autism Center is passionate about redefining autism—from disability to diversity. Help us raise awareness for autism and foster acceptance for children with autism, and stay connected through our online community.
